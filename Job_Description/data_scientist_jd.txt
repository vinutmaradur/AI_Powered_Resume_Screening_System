Job Title: Data Scientist
Company: Quantum Analytics Solutions
Location: San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
Job Type: Full-time
Job Description:
Quantum Analytics Solutions is seeking a Data Scientist to join our innovative and dynamic team. The ideal candidate will leverage advanced analytics, machine learning, and data-driven insights to solve complex business problems. This role involves working closely with cross-functional teams to drive strategic decision-making and optimize business performance.
Key Responsibilities:
•	Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets to extract meaningful insights.
•	Develop and implement machine learning models to improve business processes and customer experience.
•	Use statistical techniques to identify trends, patterns, and correlations in data.
•	Collaborate with engineering and product teams to integrate data-driven solutions into business operations.
•	Build predictive models and recommendation systems to enhance user engagement.
•	Communicate findings and insights effectively to stakeholders through data visualization and reports.
•	Stay updated with the latest industry trends, tools, and best practices in data science.
Qualifications:
•	Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field.
•	2+ years of experience in data science, analytics, or a similar role.
•	Proficiency in Python, R, or SQL for data analysis and modeling.
•	Experience with machine learning libraries such as TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, or PyTorch.
•	Strong knowledge of statistical modeling, data mining, and data visualization techniques.
•	Familiarity with cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or GCP is a plus.
•	Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
